# Diagnostic short boxes

## 80 Hz

- Best cleaned-set score.
- Strong Tdot, delta, and delta-dot profile.
- Tw is neutral rather than dominant.
- Remains first under equal weights and sensitivity checks.

## Middle-band note

- 20, 15, 10, and 40 Hz are close enough that their detailed order should not be overinterpreted.
- This is a multi-metric trade-off, not a monotonic frequency law.
